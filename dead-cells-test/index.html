Dead Cells

Oh, hey. Welcome to my personal nightmare. I’m a sentient pile of goo stuck in a prison, doomed to keep possessing some poor headless schmuck over and over again. Every time I think I’m getting somewhere—BAM! Dead. Again. And guess what? I have to start all over.

This island? Absolute hellhole. Everything here either wants to stab me, explode in my face, or turn me into a puddle of regret. Oh, and the layouts keep changing just to mess with me. Love that. At least I get some cool weapons, but let’s be real—it’s never enough.
